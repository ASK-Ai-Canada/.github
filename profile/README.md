## Our mission

ASK-Ai builds AI tooling and infrastructure in Canada, so teams get powerful, efficient AI without surrendering control of their data or their costs.

## Our commitments

- **Sovereignty** — built and hosted in Canada, keeping your operations and your data insulated from long-term geopolitical risk.
- **Security** — encrypted at rest and in transit, by default.
- **Stability** — resilient by design: backup, mirror, and failover across redundant Canadian capacity.
- **Efficiency** — our flagship ASK Token Optimizer cuts Claude Code CLI token consumption by up to 98.9%.
